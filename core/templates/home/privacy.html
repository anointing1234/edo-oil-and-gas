Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 07, 2025

Your privacy and security are of utmost importance to us. This policy outlines how we collect and use information about you in connection with our services, including our platform and website. Please read this policy alongside our Terms of Service.

1. Who Are We?

We are the Edo Oil and Gas Summit 2025, an initiative organized under the auspices of the Edo State Government. The summit is dedicated to harnessing Edo State’s natural gas potential, fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable development for Nigeria’s energy transition.

As a "data controller" under applicable Nigerian data protection laws and international standards, we are responsible for determining how we collect, store, and use your personal data.

2. How Do We Collect Your Data?

We collect information about you when you:

  • Register for the Edo Oil and Gas Summit 2025
  • Submit inquiries or applications via our website
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact us through our contact form or email
  • Visit our website and interact with our content
  • Engage with us on social media platforms

We also collect information automatically when you visit our website, using technologies such as cookies, web beacons, and similar tools. These technologies save and return information like session data, preferences, and website usage metrics for audience measurement and performance optimization.

3. What Data Do We Collect?

We collect two types of information: personal data and non-personal data.

Personal Data

This is information that identifies you, including:

Data Type Information Collected Collection Method
Summit Registration Data Full name, email address, phone number, organization, job title Summit registration form
Contact Information First name, last name, email address, phone number, message content Contact form submissions
Newsletter Subscription Email address Newsletter subscription forms
Website Analytics IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent on site Automated collection via cookies and analytics tools

Important: Do not submit sensitive personal data such as government identifiers (e.g., social security, driver’s license, or taxpayer identification numbers), complete credit card numbers, medical records, or other highly sensitive information.

Non-Personal Data

This is information that does not identify you directly. It includes aggregated or anonymized data derived from your use of our services and website, modified to remove personal identifiers.

4. How Do We Use Your Data?

We use your information for the following purposes:

For Website Visitors:

  • To enhance and improve website functionality
  • To analyze website usage and optimize user experience
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • To send newsletters and summit updates (with your consent)

For Summit Participants:

  • To confirm registrations and manage event attendance
  • To send summit-related communications and updates
  • To provide event materials and resources
  • To facilitate networking and follow-up activities

General Uses:

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security
  • To improve our services and develop new initiatives
  • To create aggregated reports for program evaluation

5. What Are Our Purposes and Legal Basis for Collecting Your Personal Data?

We collect your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you voluntarily provide information through forms, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us
  • Legitimate Interests: To operate the summit, evaluate registrations, and improve our services
  • Public Interest: To advance sustainable energy initiatives and economic development in Edo State
  • Legal Compliance: To adhere to applicable Nigerian laws and regulations

6. With Whom Do We Share Personal Data?

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only in these limited cases:

Government Partners:

  • Edo State Government: For summit administration and reporting
  • Federal Ministry of Petroleum Resources: For national energy initiatives and reporting

Service Providers:

  • Website Hosting: For hosting and technical support
  • Email Services: For sending newsletters and summit communications
  • Analytics Providers: For website performance monitoring
  • Event Management: For logistics and event coordination

Legal Requirements:

We may disclose data when required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

Data Protection Measures: We ensure third parties we share data with have appropriate data protection agreements and implement technical and organizational safeguards.

7. For How Long Do We Retain Your Personal Data?

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Summit Registrations: 2 years after the event for follow-up and impact assessment
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Contact Inquiries: 1 year after resolution
  • Website Analytics: 2 years for performance analysis

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

8. How Do We Transfer Your Data?

Your data is primarily processed and stored in Nigeria. For international transfers (e.g., cloud hosting), we ensure:

  • Standard contractual clauses are in place
  • Data encryption during transmission and storage
  • Regular security assessments of service providers
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws

9. What Are Your Rights in Connection with Personal Data?

You have the following rights under applicable data protection laws:

Access Rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Portability: Request transfer of your data in a structured format

Control Rights:

  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications (processed within 14 days)

10. Security

We prioritize the security and integrity of your personal information, using industry-standard measures to protect it during transmission and storage.

Our Security Measures Include:

  • Technical Safeguards: Encryption, secure servers, firewalls, and access controls
  • Physical Safeguards: Secure data centers with restricted access
  • Administrative Safeguards: Staff training, background checks, and confidentiality agreements
  • Regular Monitoring: Security assessments, vulnerability scans, and incident response procedures

Important Security Note: While we use robust security measures, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to protect your data using best practices but cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and analyze usage. These help us:

  • Remember preferences and settings
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior
  • Enhance website functionality
  • Deliver relevant content

You can manage cookie settings via your browser, but disabling cookies may impact website functionality.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly. For participants aged 13-17, we recommend parental guidance when providing personal information.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in practices, technology, or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated by:

  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Emailing registered users
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Changes take effect 30 days after posting. If you object, you may stop using our services and request data deletion before the new policy applies.
