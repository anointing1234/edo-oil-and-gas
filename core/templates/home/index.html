Edo Oil and Gas Summit

Energy Transition in Nigeria: Harnessing Edo State’s Natural Gas Potentials for Sustainable Development

The Edo State Government, in collaboration with the Gas Aggregator Company Nigeria Ltd/Gte (GACN), is proud to announce the Edo State Oil and Gas Summit 2025, a transformative gathering of key stakeholders and decision-makers in Nigeria’s energy sector. The summit is scheduled to take place on Thursday, 7th August 2025, at the Victor Uwaifo Creative Hub, along Benin Airport Road, Benin City, Edo State. .
